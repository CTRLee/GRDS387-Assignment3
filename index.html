Why Donacious?

With Donacious, you can now give away food without having to throw them out.

Don't Throw. Give instead.

Whether is that you cooked too much food for your dinner, or bought a 4 pack onion when you only need one, you can now give them away instead of throwing them out.

Help out people in need

There are currently 1.35 Million people living in poverty in Hong Kong who is struggling to live. Instead of throwing away your leftovers, why not help them out by giving out to people in need?

Be Part of the change

Do you know that Hong Kong generates around 3,337 tonnes of food wastes everyday? With Donacious, you can be part of the change to reduce food waste!

Your choice to handle the food

You can choose to either give away your food face to face, or drop them off at our collecting point across the city. Either way, you are helping to combat food waste.

Mutual Benefits

Donacious can help people in need and save the environment at the same time. It's like hitting two birds with one stone. So, what are you waiting for?
